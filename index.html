UNDER THE GLASS

An inside look at Patek Philippe's Self-Winding Caliber CH 28-520 C FUS.

FLYBACK CHRONOGRAPH

Unlike standard chronographs where you must stop, reset, and restart, the flyback function allows instantaneous resetting of the running second hand to zero with a single push of the button, immediately starting a new timer run.

TRAVEL TIME DUAL INDICATOR

Features two hour hands on the central axis. The lower, open-worked hour hand represents home time, while the solid hand represents local time. Two small day/night apertures indicate PM/AM in both regions.

GPS ATOMIC CALIBRATION

For our Seiko Astron selections: Connects to the GPS satellite network at the touch of a button, determining your time zone and adjusting the hands with atomic clock precision, powered purely by light energy.
