UNDER THE GLASS

An inside look at the technical marvels of the Royal Oak Jumbo.

PETITE TAPISSERIE DIAL

The guilloché dial is produced using a rare engine-turning technique on old-world pantograph machines. It forms a grid of thousands of tiny truncated pyramids that reflect light differently at every angle.

EXTRA-THIN CALIBER 7121

Specifically engineered to sit inside the extra-thin 8.1 mm case, the Caliber 7121 has a total thickness of only 3.2 mm. It houses a bidirectional rotor mounted on ball bearings and circular-grained bridges.

INTEGRATED BRACELET

A masterpiece of ergonomics and metallurgy. Composed of links decreasing in size, it fits the wrist perfectly. Every single link is meticulously hand-brushed and polished to absolute perfection.
